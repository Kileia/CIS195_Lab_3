About

Daniel Caesar is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer known for his soulful and introspective music. Since his debut in 2014, he has released two studio albums and one EP, each showcasing his unique blend of R&B, gospel, and neo-soul influences.

In addition to his studio albums and EP, Daniel Caesar has also released several singles and collaborative tracks with other artists. His music is known for its lyrical depth, heartfelt delivery, and soulful instrumentation, making him one of the most promising R&B artists of his generation.

Praise Break EP (2014)

Daniel Caesar's musical journey began with the release of his debut EP titled "Praise Break." It features seven tracks that display his exceptional vocal range and raw talent. The EP combines elements of gospel, R&B, and soul, setting the foundation for his future sound. Notable tracks include "Get You" and "Death & Taxes."

Pilgrim's Paradise EP (2015)

"Pilgrim's Paradise" is an EP by Canadian singer-songwriter Daniel Caesar, released on November 12, 2015. This soulful and introspective project, serving as a follow-up to his debut EP "Praise Break," consists of seven tracks that combine R&B, gospel, and neo-soul influences. Caesar's distinctive vocals and heartfelt lyrics explore themes of love, faith, and personal growth, creating an intimate and introspective atmosphere. Notable tracks like "Paradise," "Death & Taxes," and "Streetcar" showcase his talent and contributed to the EP's positive reception, solidifying Daniel Caesar's position as a rising star in the R&B music scene.

Freudian (2017)

Daniel Caesar's first full-length studio album, "Freudian," was released to critical acclaim. The album explores themes of love, relationships, and self-reflection, all delivered with his smooth and soulful voice. The standout tracks from this album include "Best Part" featuring H.E.R., "We Find Love," and "Blessed." "Freudian" received Grammy nominations for Best R&B Album and Best R&B Performance.

Case Study 01 (2019)

Following the success of his debut album, Daniel Caesar released his second studio album, "Case Study 01." This album delves into deeper introspection, addressing themes of personal growth, spirituality, and societal issues. The album features collaborations with artists like Brandy, Pharrell Williams, and John Mayer. Noteworthy tracks from "Case Study 01" include "Love Again" featuring Brandy, "Cyanide," and "Are You Okay?"

Never Enough (2023)

"Never Enough" is Daniel Caesar's third studio album, released on April 7, 2023, through Republic Records. The album was mostly self-recorded by Caesar during the COVID-19 pandemic. It features production from Caesar himself, Zachary Simmonds, Sir Dylan, Sevn Thomas, Rami, and Raphael Saadiq. Caesar mentioned in interviews that he explored different influences beyond traditional R&B for this album, including folk and country music. The album's title, cover art, tracklist, and release date were announced by Caesar on March 21, 2023.
